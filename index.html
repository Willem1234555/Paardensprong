Welcome to Paardensprong!

In Paardensprong, your goal is to guess a hidden word. The word is spelled out letter by letter on a 3x3 grid, but there’s a twist – the letters are arranged based on the knight's move in chess (L-shaped jumps). You can only move from one letter to another by following the knight’s movement rules. Can you figure out the word before revealing it?
